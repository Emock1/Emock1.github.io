Island University Collaborates with Smithsonian, UTMSI for Barcoding Bioblitz of Local Marine Life
This article covered a collaboration between the university, the Smithsonian's Marine Global Earth Observatory, and the University of Texas Marine Science Institute on an event called a Bioblitz. In this web story, I chose to focus on the collaborations mentioned in the story. I also wanted to show how this event was an amazing opportunity for students to be exposed to national experts in their field. I conducted email interviews with the professor and student quoted in the story. I wrote this article and it was edited by my supervisor Luisa Buttler.

A Younger Perspective: Islander Grad Students Interview Rockport Youth on Hurricane Harvey Recovery
This article was about a group of Master of Public Administration (MPA) students who, as part of their capstone project, met with and interviewed youths from Rockport High School to understand their concerns for the city after being hit by Hurricane Harvey. It heavily featured university students who, through their education, made a direct impact on a hurricane-ravaged community. I was provided already written testimonials from MPA student participants, along with the forms and presentations they used in their work. I integrated all this into the story. I wrote this article and it was edited by my supervisor Luisa Buttler.
